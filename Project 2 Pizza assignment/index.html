Pepperoni Pizza

Our classic pepperoni pizza is made from our hand tossed dough and slathered with a generous helping of our house made marinara sauce. It is then topped with freshly cut pepperoni and sprinkled with authentic mozzarella cheese. After being crisped to perfection, we garnish it with basil, oregano, and parsley.

Cheese pizza

Our quattro formaggi pizza will satisfy even the most ravenous cheese enthusiasts out there. We use only quality cheeses on this mouth-watering creation. There is nothing cheesy about our choice of cheeses for this delicacy. We top this pizza with mozzarella, fontina, feta, and parmesan cheese. Go ahead and dig into the stringy, cheesy goodness of this scrumptious dish.

Vegetarian Pizza

Don't eat meat? Fret not, we have what you're craving. Our veggie pizza has the freshest produce around. We start, as always, with our hand tossed dough and slather generous portions of our famous marinara sauce. Then we top it with mouth watering peppers, mozzarella, mushrooms, and olives. We also have non-dairy cheese available for any vegan patrons!
